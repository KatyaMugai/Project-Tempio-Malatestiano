Tempio Malatestiano in the ArCo Knowledge Graph

For this project, I selected the ArCo resource describing Tempio Malatestiano, an architectural heritage object located in Rimini, Italy.

The selected resource is:

https://w3id.org/arco/resource/ArchitecturalOrLandscapeHeritage/0800163046

Tempio Malatestiano is an interesting case for knowledge graph enrichment because it is a complex architectural and historical monument. It is not only a church, but also a cultural heritage object connected to several architectural components, historical figures, artistic objects and symbolic elements.

The goal of the project is to explore how this monument is represented in the ArCo knowledge graph and to identify possible information gaps in its RDF description.

During the exploration, I focused on two main aspects:

1. The representation of internal architectural components of the monument.
2. The representation of historical and artistic entities associated with it.

The analysis showed that ArCo already contains relevant information about Tempio Malatestiano, especially through photographic resources linked to the main architectural resource. However, some of this information is not directly represented as structured RDF relations.

For this reason, the project proposes new RDF triples and a small vocabulary extension in order to make this information more explicit, machine-readable and reusable.
